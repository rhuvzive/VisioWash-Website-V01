Process

How VisioWash Works

Our proprietary AI pipeline scans, maps, and cleans your vehicle in one seamless pass — no human error, no missed spots.

01

Vehicle Scan

Eight high-resolution cameras capture a 360° image of your vehicle in under two seconds. Our CV model identifies dirt, grime, and contaminants at the pixel level.

02

Dirt Mapping

The AI generates a heatmap overlay of contaminated zones — from heavy mud splashes to microscopic brake dust — and routes the cleaning arms accordingly.

03

Precision Clean

Robotic arms and targeted spray nozzles focus cleaning power on detected zones. Clean areas receive only a gentle rinse — saving water and protecting paint.
